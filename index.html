Turtle Facts

Here are a few cool facts about turtles
I hope you like them!

Older than dinosaurs?

Turtles date back to more than 200 years ago, older than most dinosaurs!

All shapes and sizes

There are more than 300 species of turtles, the largest being the leatherback turtle and the smallest being the speckled padloper tortoise, weighing a measly 165g

How long can they stay under water?

Believe it or not, turtles can stay under the water for more than five hours!

How do they drink?

Turtles have special glands in their bodies which seperate the salt from water when they drink!

Shells

Turtles also have a lower shell called a 'plastron'

Can all turtles retract their bodies?

Contrast to popular beliefs, not all turtles can retract their heads, and, those who do, can only do so at varying degrees.
